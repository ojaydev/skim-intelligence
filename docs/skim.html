Anthropic Opus 4.7 Hackathon  ·  April 2026

Autonomous prediction market
intelligence

Skim Intelligence is a five-agent system powered by Claude Opus 4.7 that autonomously scans live prediction market orderbooks, reasons about structural yield opportunities, paper-trades approved signals, and attributes performance at every epoch close.

It makes no directional bets. Every edge it extracts — maker rebates, liquidity rewards, mint/burn arbitrage — is available regardless of whether markets resolve YES or NO.

Core Insight
"Prediction markets pay bots to exist. Maker rebates, liquidity rewards, and mint/burn arbitrage are structural edges baked into platform design. Most operators access them through hardcoded rules. Skim uses Opus 4.7 to reason about each situation dynamically — the way a sophisticated quantitative analyst would."
Three Strategy Layers
01
Mint / Burn Arbitrage
A YES share + NO share = $1.00 by protocol invariant. When the complement sum drifts above or below $1.00 by more than the taker fee, a risk-free arb exists. Alpha Agent checks both legs simultaneously and rejects if data is stale.
net margin > 3.2% required
02
CLOB Market Making
Post two-sided limit orders at ±3¢ from mid, capturing the bid-ask spread on fills. Zero maker fees. Inventory neutrality enforced — YES and NO positions stay within ±15% of each other by notional to avoid directional drift.
spread > 3% · depth > $200
03
Liquidity Reward Farming
Bayse pays from a fixed pool per market for resting two-sided orders. Market-making orders automatically qualify when two_sided_eligible = true. Incremental yield on top of spread capture — no additional capital required.
pool > $50 · yield est. +1.2%
The Five Agents
Scanner
Deterministic · No LLM
Trigger: WebSocket stream (Bayse + Polymarket)
Holds persistent WS connections to wss://relay.bayse.markets/ws/market-data and wss://ws-subscriptions-clob.polymarket.com/ws/market. On each book delta, computes mid price, spread %, depth, reward eligibility, and complement sum. Writes MarketSnapshot to Cloudflare KV.
Alpha
claude-opus-4-7
Trigger: Per market snapshot
The core intelligence. Receives the full market snapshot and reasons through all three strategy layers simultaneously. Outputs a structured signal with opportunity_score (0–1), per-strategy plans, and full thinking text streamed live to the dashboard. This reasoning IS the demo.
Risk
claude-opus-4-7
Trigger: Per Alpha signal
The circuit breaker. Enforces hard limits — daily loss cap, per-market exposure, total portfolio exposure, data freshness (rejects if snapshot > 60s old), inventory imbalance, execution mode. Returns approved / modified / rejected with full reasoning.
Execution
Deterministic · Paper Engine
Trigger: Per approved signal
Pure state machine. Simulates order placement, applies realistic fill probability based on orderbook depth and volume, deducts fees, tracks positions and P&L in Cloudflare D1. Runs inventory checks every 60s. Closes epochs every 5 min.
Reporter
claude-opus-4-7
Trigger: Every 5-minute epoch close
Receives the full P&L breakdown and generates a plain-English attribution summary bucketed into spread capture / liquidity rewards / arb profits / fees. Produces the shareable epoch card. Always labels results as "paper trading" — never hides negative epochs.

WebSocket Data Layer
Polymarket wss://ws-subscriptions-clob.polymarket.com/ws/market Public · no auth · book deltas, price_change, best_bid_ask
Bayse wss://relay.bayse.markets/ws/market-data HMAC-SHA256 auth · orderbook snapshots, activity feed
Dashboard wss://skim.workers.dev/api/ws Durable Object hibernation API · signals, fills, epoch events

The Scanner Durable Object holds the two upstream connections. Outgoing WS connections do not support hibernation — the Scanner stays resident while live. Incoming dashboard connections use ctx.acceptWebSocket() (Hibernation API) to avoid billing duration during idle. Bootstrap pattern: fetch REST snapshot on connect, then apply incremental book deltas. On reconnect, re-fetch — no sequence numbers on Polymarket.

Hackathon Context

Built in 5 days for the Anthropic Opus 4.7 Hackathon (submission deadline: April 26, 2026, 8 PM EST). The challenge theme: "Build From What You Know." Skim Intelligence is the intelligence layer of Skim — a Bayse-native liquidity engine for African prediction markets.

The demo centres on the Alpha Agent's live reasoning stream. Opus 4.7 analyses each market out loud — assessing spread quality, arb viability, inventory balance, and reward yield in real time. That transparency is the product differentiator: not a black box, but legible, auditable intelligence.

Licensed MIT. All agent prompts, paper trading engine, and orchestration logic are open source after submission.
